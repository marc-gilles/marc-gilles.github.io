I started a new postdoc in the lab of [Emily Burdfield-Steel](https://emilyburdfieldsteel.com/) at the [University of Amsterdam](https://ibed.uva.nl/)!

<!--more-->

I will investigate whether avian predators make irrational decisions when foraging, and how this could affect the selection on their prey. For this, I will be studying great tits at [Lammi Biological Station](https://www.helsinki.fi/en/research-stations/lammi-biological-station/research/research-themes) in Finland together with Johanna Mappes (University of Helsinki). Exciting times ahead!
